F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does DevServices offer?

We provide a wide range of services, including website development, mobile app development (iOS and Android), bug bounty programs, graphic design, SEO and ads management, database management, and cybersecurity solutions, among many others.

How long has DevServices been in business?

We have been in the business for over 10 years, delivering top-quality software services to clients worldwide.

Do you provide custom software development?

Yes, we specialize in developing custom software solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Do you offer remote internships?

Absolutely! We offer remote internships where interns can work on real-world projects, collaborate with our team, and gain hands-on experience in their chosen field. These internships often lead to paid opportunities within DevServices.

Can I work from anywhere in the world?

Yes, DevServices embraces remote work, allowing you to work from anywhere in the world. Our team members are spread across various time zones, and we use digital tools to collaborate effectively.

What are the working hours for remote employees?

DevServices offers flexible working hours to accommodate different time zones. However, some projects may require occasional meetings during specific timeframes, so we encourage a balance of flexibility and availability.
